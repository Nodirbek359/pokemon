Everything You Need To Know About IMG Worlds of Adventure

Updated: March 22, 2023 11 min read
2503

Dubai is home to some of the best theme parks in the world, including IMG Worlds of Adventure, an exciting indoor theme park spanning a space of 1.5 million square feet, making it one of the biggest theme parks in the world.

IMG Worlds of Adventure offers visitors an unparalleled entertainment experience, with five themed zones featuring an array of rides, attractions, and live shows based on popular characters from your childhood, including Cartoon Network and Marvel characters. In this article, we will explore the park’s offerings, history, and what makes it a unique attraction in Dubai.

Fun Facts About IMG Worlds of Adventure

  • IMG Worlds of Adventure is the second-largest indoor theme park in the world. 
  • IMG Worlds of Adventure houses five adventure zones – Marvel, Cartoon Network, Lost Valley, IMG Boulevard, and the Kids Zone.
  • There are 20+ exciting rides, attractions, and shows based on famous characters, including Ben10, Spiderman, and Adventure Time, inside IMG Worlds of Adventure.
  • IMG Worlds of Adventure features an exciting haunted house experience perfect for anyone looking for a spooky yet fun experience.
  • Visitors can meet their favorite characters in special meets and greets throughout the park. Keep an eye out for Spiderman!
  • IMG Worlds of Adventure also houses a cinema where visitors can enjoy the latest movies.
  • The IMG Kids Zone is a large inflatable park perfect for keeping the young ones entertained all day, equipped with climbing walls, a football pitch, slides, a sweeper game, and many more attractions.

The Five Adventure Zones at IMG Worlds of Adventure

Marvel Zone

The Marvel Zone is a superhero-themed zone that will delight fans of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Here, visitors can experience a range of thrilling rides and attractions based on popular characters such as Iron Man, Thor, Spider-Man, and the Avengers.

The zone features various attractions, including:

Avengers: Battle of Ultron – This ride takes visitors on a 3D motion simulator journey where they join the Avengers in a battle against Ultron.

Spider-Man: Doc Ock’s Revenge – This attraction takes visitors on a swinging adventure with Spider-Man as they try to stop Doctor Octopus from stealing a fusion reactor.

Hulk: Epsilon Base 3D – This ride uses 3D technology to immerse visitors in a world where they help the Hulk fight off alien invaders.

Cartoon Network

The Cartoon Network Zone features popular characters from Cartoon Network shows such as Adventure Time, The Powerpuff Girls, and Ben 10. One of the most popular attractions in the Cartoon Network Zone is the LazyTown Playground, based on the popular children’s TV show. This playground features a range of interactive activities, including climbing walls, slides, and a soft play area, that will keep children entertained for hours.

This zone is suitable for visitors of all ages and includes attractions such as:

The Amazing Ride of Gumball – This ride takes visitors on a roller coaster journey through the town of Elmore, where they can help Gumball and Darwin save the town from destruction.

The Powerpuff Girls – Mojo Jojo’s Robot Rampage – This ride takes visitors on a spinning adventure as they try to help the Powerpuff Girls defeat Mojo Jojo.

Ben 10: 5D Hero Time – This attraction uses 5D technology to immerse visitors in the world of Ben 10, where they help him fight off alien invaders.

The Lost Valley

The Lost Valley Zone is a dinosaur-themed area featuring over 60 animatronic dinosaurs. This zone takes visitors back to the Jurassic period, where they can experience the thrill of riding some of the world’s most terrifying creatures.

One of the most impressive attractions in the Lost Valley Zone is the Dino Carousel, which features life-size dinosaur models that move up and down as visitors ride around in circles. Another popular attraction is the Adventure Fortress, a multi-level play area with climbing walls, slides, and a ball pit.

Other attractions in this zone include:

The Velociraptor – This ride takes visitors on a high-speed roller coaster ride through a prehistoric jungle, featuring multiple twists and turns.

Forbidden Territory – This attraction is a virtual reality experience where visitors can explore the world of dinosaurs from the safety of a jeep.

Dino Carousel – This attraction is a dinosaur-themed carousel suitable for younger visitors.

IMG Boulevard

The IMG Boulevard Zone is the park’s main entrance and features a range of restaurants, cafes, and shops. This zone is designed to be a hub for visitors, providing them a place to relax and recharge between rides and attractions. The zone features a range of dining options, including international cuisine and fast food, as well as souvenir shops and retail outlets.

Some of the attractions in this zone include:

Haunted Hotel – This attraction is a walk-through haunted house suitable for older visitors.

The Predator – This ride takes visitors on a high-speed journey through a futuristic city, featuring multiple twists and turns.

Novo Cinemas – This cinema features the latest blockbuster movies and is a perfect way to relax after a day of rides and attractions.

IMG Kids Zone

IMG Kids Zone is the latest addition to the theme park and features a huge inflatable adventure playground dedicated to young guests. The zone spans an area of 17,000+ square feet and features many exciting things to do for kids, including wall climbing, football pitches, slides, and many other exciting adventures that will keep the little ones busy all day.

*IMG Kids Zone is not included in the entrance ticket. Guests must pre-book at the attraction for a cover charge: One hour – AED 50 | Full day – AED 100

The Best Rides at IMG Worlds of Adventure Dubai

Velociraptor Rollercoaster:

The Velociraptor rollercoaster is one of the most popular rides at IMG Worlds of Adventure Dubai. It is a high-speed coaster that reaches speeds of up to 60 miles per hour. The ride features a range of twists, turns, and drops that are sure to thrill visitors. The coaster is designed to simulate the experience of being chased by a pack of velociraptors, which adds to the ride’s excitement.

The Haunted Hotel:

The Haunted Hotel is a spooky attraction that takes visitors on a walking tour through a haunted hotel. The attraction features a range of spooky characters and jumps scares that will fright visitors. The attention to detail in the Haunted Hotel is impressive, with realistic sets and special effects that make visitors feel like they are really in a haunted hotel.

Predator Rollercoaster

The Predator rollercoaster is another high-speed coaster at IMG Worlds of Adventure Dubai. It features a range of twists and turns that will excite visitors. The ride is designed to simulate the experience of being hunted by a predator, which adds to the thrill of the ride.

Avengers: Battle of Ultron

The Avengers: Battle of Ultron ride takes visitors on a high-speed adventure through the streets of New York City. The ride features a range of special effects and animatronics that bring the world of the Avengers to life. Visitors will feel like they are part of the action as they battle the evil Ultron and his army of robots.

The Amazing Ride of Gumball

The Amazing Ride of Gumball is a ride that takes visitors on a journey through the world of The Amazing World of Gumball. The ride features a range of special effects and animatronics that bring the world of Gumball to life. Visitors will feel like they are part of the show as they ride through the colorful world of Gumball.

Spider-Man Doc Ock’s Revenge

The Spider-Man Doc Ock’s Revenge ride is a thrilling experience that allows visitors to swing through the city alongside Spider-Man as he battles the villainous Doctor Octopus. The ride features a range of special effects and animatronics that bring the world of Spider-Man to life. Visitors will feel like they are part of the action as they swing through the city and battle villains.

Ben 10: 5D Cinema

The Ben 10: 5D Cinema is a cinema experience that allows visitors to join Ben 10 on a thrilling adventure through space. The ride features a range of special effects and 5D technology that makes visitors feel like they are really part of the action. The Ben 10: 5D Cinema is a must-see attraction for fans of the popular cartoon series.

Adventure Time: The Ride of OOO

The Adventure Time: The Ride of OOO is a ride that takes visitors on a journey through the Land of Ooo, the setting of the popular TV show Adventure Time. The ride features a range of special effects and animatronics that bring the world of Adventure Time to life. Visitors will feel like they are part of the show as they ride through the colorful world of Adventure Time.

Things To Know Before Visiting IMG Worlds of Adventure

  • IMG Worlds of Adventure Timing: IMG Worlds of Adventure is open daily,  from noon to 10 p.m., Sunday to Friday, and noon to 11 p.m. on Saturday.
  • IMG Worlds of Adventure Dress Code: Casual, appropriate clothing is allowed at IMG Worlds of Adventure. We recommend wearing something comfortable as you will spend a long time in the park.
  • Children up to 1.05m in height can enter IMG Park for free.
  • Tickets to IMG Worlds of Adventure are valid for 30 days from the date of purchase.
  • Pregnant women are not recommended to participate in the attractions at IMG Worlds Of Adventure for safety purposes.
  • Accessibility: Guests with registered disabilities can enter the theme park on a half-discounted-price day pass when providing proof of disability; the helper can enter the theme park upon purchase of a full-priced day pass.
  • Facilities included at the park: Free Parking, ATMs, First Aid, Toilets, Wheelchair, and Stroller Hire

Things To Do Around IMG Worlds of Adventure

Global Village

If you are looking for other fun things to do in Dubai, Global Village is the perfect addition to your trip. Global Village Dubai is an annual cultural extravaganza and entertainment destination in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It is a massive outdoor theme park that brings together cultures from all around the world in a vibrant and colorful celebration.

The park is divided into various pavilions, each showcasing a different country or culture, with traditional food, music, clothing, and handicrafts on display. The pavilions are designed to look like mini replicas of famous landmarks worldwide, such as the Eiffel Tower, Big Ben, and the Taj Mahal.

In addition to the cultural exhibits, Global Village Dubai also features a wide range of entertainment options for all ages, including live music performances, circus shows, street performances, thrill rides, and games. Visitors can enjoy the main stage shows, street performances, and other live shows throughout the park.

Various dining options are also available, including traditional cuisines from different countries, fast food, and other snacks. Visitors can also enjoy shopping for souvenirs and handicrafts from around the world.

Miracle Garden

Miracle Garden is another exciting venue to add to your Dubai itinerary. Miracle Garden is a magnificent floral attraction and is one of the world’s largest flower gardens. This garden is a true masterpiece of horticulture, featuring more than 45 million flowers of different species and varieties spread over 72,000 square meters of land.

The garden is a seasonal attraction that is open from November to May every year, and it offers a visual feast for visitors with its vast collection of flowers, plants, and structures. It’s designed with impressive floral displays, including floral castles, arches, and sculptures, making it a popular tourist destination in Dubai.

The garden’s landscape is changed yearly to keep the attraction fresh and exciting for visitors. Each season, the garden displays a new theme with different arrangements of flowers, such as hearts, stars, and pyramids, which add to the attraction’s appeal.

In addition to the vibrant floral displays, the garden features several attractions, including a butterfly garden, a trampoline park, a live music stage, and numerous food and beverage outlets. There are also various photo opportunities for visitors to capture their moments in the garden.
